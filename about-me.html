About me

Hi, I'm Ann.

Writing is my passion and advocacy is my mission. That is, advocacy for anything humanitarian especially sharing to the world socially relevant stories that are told truthfully, accurately and compellingly. In my 13 years of journalism experience, no other topic has touched my soul.

Like you, I strive to keep learning and practising. After all, as Sir Arthur Conan Doyle once said: “Education never ends, Watson.”

In journalism, it couldn’t be any truer.

Thanks for stopping by and keep writing!
